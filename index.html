A month of deep dives into sci-fi classics, discovering new indie artists, and tracking the stories that matter. Here's what stood out.

Books

Dune by Frank Herbert 9/10
Science Fiction
An epic tale of politics, religion, and ecology on a desert planet. The world-building is unmatched, and the themes about resource scarcity feel incredibly relevant today.
Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir
Science Fiction
A gripping space survival story with clever science and an unexpectedly touching friendship. Couldn't put it down.
